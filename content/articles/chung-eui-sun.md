## Background

- **Full name (Korean)**: 정의선
- **Family / affiliation**: Member of the founding family behind Hyundai Motor Group.
- **Education**: Studied in Korea and the United States (commonly reported: Korea University; University of San Francisco for graduate study).

## Roles (high-level)

- Executive leader within **Hyundai Motor Group**.
- Widely reported as having served as **Executive Chair** of Hyundai Motor Group (formal leadership transition from his father, Chung Mong-koo).
