## Background

- Byju Raveendran is known publicly as the founder of the edtech company **Byju's** (Think & Learn Pvt. Ltd.).[^1]
- Media coverage around the company has often focused both on rapid growth in online tutoring/test-prep and later on governance, financing, and legal disputes.[^1]

## What He Is Known For

### Education and early work (high-level)

- Public biographies generally describe him as having an early reputation for teaching and test-prep coaching before building a productized learning business.[^1]

### Building Byju's

- Byju's became one of the most visible Indian edtech brands during the global expansion of consumer learning apps.
- The company grew via a combination of mobile-first learning content and acquisitions, and it raised capital from multiple investors over time.[^1]

## Controversies and Public Scrutiny

- From 2022 onward, reporting about Byju's increasingly highlighted **audits, delayed filings, creditor disputes, and restructuring/insolvency-related proceedings** in various jurisdictions.[^1]
- Allegations and counter-allegations appeared in public reporting; specific claims often vary by source and have been contested by different parties.[^1]
