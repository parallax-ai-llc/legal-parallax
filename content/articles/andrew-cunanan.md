## Summary

Spree killer who murdered five people including fashion designer Gianni Versace before killing himself.

## Early life and background

- Born in **National City, California** (U.S.).
- Reported to have been raised primarily in the **San Diego** area.
- Known in contemporary reporting as a person who often used **aliases** and presented differing stories about his education, finances, and social connections.

## 1997 murder spree (high-level)

Between April and July 1997, Cunanan killed **five people** in multiple U.S. cities, culminating in the killing of fashion designer **Gianni Versace** in Miami Beach.

- The victims are commonly identified as:
  - **Jeffrey Trail** (Minneapolis, Minnesota)
  - **David Madson** (near Rush Lake, Minnesota)
  - **Lee Miglin** (Chicago, Illinois)
  - **William Reese** (Pennsville, New Jersey)
  - **Gianni Versace** (Miami Beach, Florida)

## Investigation and manhunt

- The killings triggered an intensive **multi-state investigation**.
- After Versace was killed, law enforcement conducted a large-scale **manhunt** in the Miami area.
- Cunanan was later found dead of an apparent **suicide** in Miami Beach.

## Public impact and legacy

- The killing of Versace drew **international media attention** and continues to be a reference point in discussions of celebrity-targeted violence.
- The case has been revisited in **books, documentaries, and dramatizations**, shaping public perception of the event and of Cunanan.
