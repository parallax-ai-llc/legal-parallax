## Background and Public Profile

- Known in U.S. media as the "QAnon Shaman" for his distinctive costume (horned headdress, face paint, and flag/spear-like prop) and prominent presence during the January 6, 2021 attack on the U.S. Capitol.
- Became a widely circulated visual symbol of the riot through photos and broadcast footage.

## January 6, 2021 (U.S. Capitol Attack)

High-level points that are commonly reported:

- Entered the U.S. Capitol building during the breach.
- Was photographed inside the building, including in and around Senate-related areas.
- Left behind written messages and participated in chanting/performative acts that drew extensive media coverage.

## Legal Proceedings (High-Level)
