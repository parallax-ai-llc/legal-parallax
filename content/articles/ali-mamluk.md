## Additional Context (High-Level)

### Background

- Syrian intelligence and security official associated with the Assad government.
- Often described in reporting as part of the core security establishment during the Syrian civil war.
- Some sources give a more specific birth date (19 February 1946) than the placeholder used above.

### Senior Roles (Selected)

- Linked to senior roles within Syria's intelligence apparatus, including the General Intelligence/General Security Directorate.
- Reported to have led the National Security Bureau beginning in 2012, coordinating across security services.
- Appointed to a senior security post as Deputy Vice President for Security Affairs in 2019 (per widely cited public reporting).
- In 2024, also reported as holding an advisory role to the presidency on national security and military affairs.

### Sanctions and Allegations

- International sanctions have been imposed in connection with alleged involvement in repression and serious human rights abuses.
- Allegations commonly cited include overseeing or enabling detention and torture by security services; these are frequently referenced in sanction designations and human rights reporting.
