## Background

- Betts was born in 1994 and grew up in Ohio.
- Public reporting has described a history of behavioral and disciplinary issues during his school years.
- Prior to the attack, he lived in the Dayton, Ohio area.

## Dayton Mass Shooting (2019)

- Date: 2019-08-04 (early morning).
- Location: Oregon District entertainment area, Dayton, Ohio.
- Weapon: AR-15–style pistol (as described in contemporary reporting) and additional magazines.
- Casualties: 9 people killed (not including the perpetrator) and multiple others injured.
- Among those killed was Betts’s sister, who was with him at the time.
- Police officers shot and killed Betts within roughly a minute of the first shots being fired, according to official accounts.

## Aftermath and Investigation

- Authorities examined potential motives, including interpersonal and ideological factors, but no single definitive motive was publicly confirmed.
- The incident prompted renewed public discussion in the United States about gun violence, policing response times, and firearm regulations.
- The city of Dayton and local organizations held vigils and provided victim-support and counseling services.
