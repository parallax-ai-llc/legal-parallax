## Summary
Payton Gendron killed 10 Black people at a supermarket in Buffalo, New York on May 14, 2022 in a racially motivated attack. He livestreamed the shooting. Sentenced to life without parole.

## 2022 Buffalo Supermarket Shooting (High-Level)
- **Date/Location:** May 14, 2022; a supermarket in Buffalo, New York.
- **Victims:** 10 people were killed; additional people were injured.
- **Method:** Firearm attack; the assault was livestreamed online.
- **Targeting:** The attack has been characterized in public sources as racially motivated.

## Legal Proceedings
