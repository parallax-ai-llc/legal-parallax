## Background

- Wikipedia identifies him as a former British Army soldier and network engineer.
- His mother was reportedly born in Iran.
- His date of birth is widely reported as **27 September 2001** (note: this may differ from simplified dates used in some datasets).

## Allegations and charges (high-level)

- Reported allegations include:
  - contact with Iranian intelligence and passing on information;
  - unlawful access to personal information about British soldiers;
  - sharing classified or sensitive documents.
- Reported charges include breaching the UK **Official Secrets Act**, escaping lawful custody, and terrorism-related charges (as described by Wikipedia).

## Prison escape (HM Prison Wandsworth)

- The escape occurred on **6 September 2023** from HM Prison Wandsworth in London (reported).
- The incident triggered a high-profile search and public disruption at major transport hubs.
- He was recaptured by the Metropolitan Police after several days on the run.

## Legal outcome (as reported)

- Wikipedia reports a prison sentence of **14 years and 3 months** and lists his status as incarcerated.
- Reporting around the case includes a guilty verdict for spying for Iran in **November 2024**.
