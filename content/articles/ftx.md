## What It Was

- **Type:** Cryptocurrency exchange and related corporate group (FTX Trading Ltd. and affiliates).[^1]
- **Core products (high level):** Spot trading and derivatives products (including perpetual futures), plus ancillary services typical of centralized exchanges (custody-like account balances, internal ledgers, and off-exchange rails).[^1]
- **Public profile:** Became widely visible through aggressive marketing, sponsorships, and media coverage during the 2021 bull market.[^1]

## Why It Matters (High-Level)

- **Trust and custody risk:** The collapse is frequently cited as a case study in the risks of centralized custody, opaque balance sheets, and weak internal controls.[^1]
- **Regulatory and market impact:** The failure intensified calls for stronger regulation, improved exchange transparency, and clearer segregation of customer funds.[^1]
- **Industry lesson:** “Name recognition” and sponsorships are not substitutes for audited financials, robust governance, or verifiable reserves.[^1]
