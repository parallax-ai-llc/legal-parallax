## Summary

G. V. Prakash Kumar (born 13 June 1987) is an Indian composer, playback singer, actor, and film producer who works mainly in Tamil cinema.[^1]

High-level notes:
- Debuted as a film composer with *Veyil* (2006).[^1]
- Became especially prominent in the early 2010s through a run of widely discussed soundtracks and background scores.[^1]
- Expanded into acting with *Darling* (2015).[^1]
- Has received major industry recognition, including multiple National Film Awards (as listed on Wikipedia).[^1]
