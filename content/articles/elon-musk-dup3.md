## High-level Overview

- Elon Musk (born 1971) is a business executive and entrepreneur known for founding and leading multiple technology and aerospace companies.
- He has been closely associated with commercialization of electric vehicles, private space launch services, and large-scale satellite internet.

## Major Companies and Roles (Selected)

- **Zip2**: Early internet company; one of Musk's first notable startups (1990s).
- **X.com / PayPal**: Online payments company that became PayPal after mergers and rebranding; later acquired by eBay (2002).
- **SpaceX**: Founded in 2002; develops reusable rockets and spacecraft and provides commercial launch services.
- **Tesla, Inc.**: Electric vehicle and energy company; Musk is widely known as a key leader of Tesla (CEO in modern era).
- **SolarCity / Tesla Energy**: Solar and energy storage efforts consolidated under Tesla after SolarCity acquisition.
- **Neuralink**: Neurotechnology company working on brain–computer interface research.
- **The Boring Company**: Infrastructure and tunneling company.
- **Twitter / X**: Musk acquired Twitter in 2022; the platform was later rebranded as X.
- **xAI**: Artificial intelligence company announced in 2023.
