## Summary

Greek crime journalist shot dead outside his Athens home.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 2021 | Shot dead outside his home |

## Reporting Focus and Work

- Investigative journalist known for covering **organized crime** and police/crime beats in Greece.
- Worked with the television broadcaster **Star Channel**.
- Founded the news site **Bloko** (bloko.gr), which published crime and policing-related reporting.

## Assassination (2021)

- Karaivaz was shot and killed on **9 April 2021** near his home in the Athens suburb of **Alimos**.
- Reporting described the killing as an **execution-style** attack; early public assessments pointed to links with **organized crime**.

## Investigation and Aftermath

- Greek police reportedly examined his **computer, car, and mobile phone** as part of the investigation.
- Public reporting described a suspected surveillance component: one group allegedly tracked his movements and informed the shooters.
- Investigators were reported to have reviewed phone-call data and security-camera footage from multiple locations near the crime scene.

## Reactions and Media-Freedom Context

- Political leaders in Greece issued public condolences following the killing.
- European officials, including the President of the European Commission, publicly condemned the assassination.
- The **European Federation of Journalists** announced plans to send a mission to Greece in connection with concerns about journalist safety and media freedom.
