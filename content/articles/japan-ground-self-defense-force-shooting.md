## Summary

An 18-year-old JGSDF trainee opened fire at a shooting range in Gifu Prefecture, killing 2 instructors and injuring 1. One of the extremely rare firearms incidents in Japan's military.

## What Happened (High-Level)

- **Where**: A JGSDF shooting range in **Gifu Prefecture**, Japan.
- **Who**: An **18-year-old trainee** (a new member of the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force).
- **Outcome**: **2 instructors were killed** and **1 person was injured**.
- **Why it mattered**: Firearms incidents of this kind are **very rare** in Japan, including within the JSDF.

## Context: Live-Fire Training in the JGSDF

- The JGSDF is the **ground warfare branch** of Japan’s Self-Defense Forces.
- Basic and unit training includes **weapons handling** and **live-fire drills** under supervision.
- Japan has **strict civilian gun controls**, but the JSDF maintains and trains with firearms as part of national defense.
