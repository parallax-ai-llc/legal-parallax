## Context: 1990 Jamaat al Muslimeen coup attempt

- The Jamaat al Muslimeen coup attempt took place **27 July – 1 August 1990** in Trinidad and Tobago.[^2]
- The group held hostages at the **Red House** (seat of Parliament) and at the headquarters of state-owned television broadcaster **TTT**.[^2]
- The attempt ended with the insurgents’ **surrender on 1 August 1990**; they were charged with treason, and later ordered released by the Court of Appeal.[^2]
- Reporting on leadership of the attempt commonly names **Yasin Abu Bakr** and **Bilaal Abdullah** among the leaders.[^2]
