## Context: ASWJ / Ansar al-Sunna in Mozambique

- **Local usage**: ASWJ is commonly expanded as *Ahlu Sunna wal Jamaa* (also rendered *Ahl al-Sunna wal-Jama'a*), a label used by the insurgent movement in **Cabo Delgado Province, northern Mozambique**.
- **Common names**: Reporting and open sources often refer to the group as **Ansar al-Sunna** or **al-Shabaab (Mozambique)** (distinct from Somalia-based al-Shabaab).
- **Conflict setting**: The movement is associated with the broader **Cabo Delgado insurgency**, which began in **2017** and has involved mass displacement and repeated attacks on towns and infrastructure.

## High-level operational pattern (group-level)

- The insurgency has included **raids on district capitals and villages**, temporary seizures of territory, and attacks on security forces and civilians.
- International coverage has highlighted major incidents during **2020–2021**, including fighting around **Palma** in Cabo Delgado.
