### Background & Education

- Indra Krishnamurthy Nooyi (born 1955) is an Indian-American business executive best known for leading PepsiCo.
- She grew up in Chennai (Madras), India, and later studied management and strategy.
- Commonly cited education: Madras Christian College; IIM Calcutta (PG diploma); and Yale School of Management (MBA).

### PepsiCo (high-level timeline)

- Joined PepsiCo in the 1990s and held senior strategy roles before becoming CEO.
- CEO tenure is widely cited as 2006–2018; she also served as board chair for part of that period.
- Her leadership is often associated with portfolio shifts toward "better-for-you" offerings and long-term brand building.

### Themes associated with her work

- Corporate strategy and M&A/portfolio management at scale.
- Global consumer packaged goods (CPG) operations and supply-chain complexity.
- Governance, leadership development, and cross-cultural management.
