## Key Facts

- **Full name**: Alisher Burkhanovich Usmanov.[^1]
- **Background**: Born in the Uzbek SSR (then part of the Soviet Union); later became one of Russia’s wealthiest industrialists and investors.[^1]
- **Primary businesses**: Built wealth through mining/metals and telecom/media holdings; associated with USM/Metalloinvest and stakes in major Russian companies.[^1]
- **International constraints**: Targeted by multiple Western sanctions regimes starting in 2022 amid Russia’s invasion of Ukraine.[^1]

## Early Life & Education

- Born in 1953 in Chust, Uzbek SSR.[^1]
- Studied international law at the **Moscow State Institute of International Relations (MGIMO)**.[^1]

## Business & Holdings (High-Level)

Usmanov’s public profile is most often linked to a mix of **metals/mining**, **telecommunications**, and **media/investment** activity:

- **Metals & mining**: Widely associated with **Metalloinvest**, a major iron ore and steel producer.[^1]
- **Telecom**: Associated with major telecom holdings/investments, including ties to **MegaFon** (historically reported as part of his business sphere).[^1]
- **Media & other investments**: Reported involvement with various media and investment structures over time.[^1]

## Sports & Public Visibility

- Formerly known internationally for investment ties to English football club **Arsenal FC** (historical shareholding reported in public sources).[^1]

## Sanctions & Restrictions

- **EU sanctions (2022)**: Sanctioned by the European Union in the context of the Ukraine war and alleged proximity to the Russian leadership.[^1]
- **UK sanctions (2022)**: Sanctioned by the United Kingdom under its Russia sanctions framework.[^1]
- Sanctions coverage typically includes **asset freezes** and **travel restrictions**, depending on jurisdiction.[^1]
