## Background (High-Level)

- Gerald Eugene Stano was born in 1951 in the United States.
- He is most commonly associated with murders committed in Florida.
- Public sources describe him as a transient offender whose crimes spanned multiple jurisdictions, complicating investigations.

## Crimes and Confessions (Overview)

- Stano became widely known for giving multiple confessions to homicide cases.
- He confessed to a large number of murders (often cited as 41), though the number of cases definitively attributed to him can vary by source and evidentiary standard.
- Multi-jurisdiction cases often require:
  - Corroboration beyond confession
  - Consistent timelines and victim identification
  - Physical or documentary evidence linking suspect to specific events

## Investigation and Legal Outcome

- Stano was arrested and prosecuted in Florida.
- He was convicted of murder and ultimately sentenced to death.
- He was executed in 1998.
