### Business Strategy (Red Bull)

- **Brand-first positioning:** Sold an "energy" identity rather than a beverage commodity.
- **Distribution + presence:** Prioritized availability in nightlife, sports, and youth-oriented venues where early adopters concentrated.
- **Event-led marketing:** Sponsored and created events that produced repeatable, shareable stories.
- **Media flywheel:** Investments in sports and content created owned channels to amplify the brand.
