## Summary

Greek neo-Nazi politician and Golden Dawn member convicted of leading a criminal organization.

Kasidiaris is best known internationally as a prominent spokesperson and parliamentarian associated with Greece’s far-right Golden Dawn party, and for high-profile public controversies linked to violence and hate speech allegations.
