## Background

- Saudi Arabian security and intelligence official.
- Known publicly primarily through reporting and official statements around the 2018 Jamal Khashoggi case.

## Role and Public Profile

- Served as a senior figure associated with Saudi intelligence structures.
- Became internationally known after being named in connection with the Khashoggi killing in Istanbul (2018).

## Connection to the Khashoggi Case (High-Level)

- Jamal Khashoggi, a Saudi journalist and Washington Post columnist, was killed at the Saudi consulate in Istanbul on 2 Oct 2018.
- Al Assiri was identified by Saudi authorities and international reporting as one of the officials linked to the operation.
- Saudi Arabia announced a series of detentions and dismissals in the aftermath; Al Assiri was among those removed from roles during that period.
