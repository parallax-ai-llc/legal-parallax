## Background

- Identified in open reporting as a French national associated with al-Qaeda-linked networks in Syria.
- Described as a bomb maker / explosives specialist rather than a public-facing spokesman.

## Role and Activities (as described in open sources)

- Reported to have worked on improvised explosive devices (IEDs) and other bomb-making activities.
- Frequently mentioned in connection with the so-called "Khorasan Group" label used in some U.S. reporting about veteran al-Qaeda operatives in Syria.
- In broader reporting, this network was described as operating alongside al-Nusra Front (Jabhat al-Nusra) during the Syrian civil war period.

## Reported Death

- Open sources report that he was killed in a U.S. airstrike in Syria in 2015.
- Reporting typically frames the strike as targeting al-Qaeda-linked operatives involved in external plotting and/or specialized capabilities.
