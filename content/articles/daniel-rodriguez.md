## Background

Public biographical details about Rodriguez are limited in commonly cited sources. He is generally described in reporting as a U.S. citizen who traveled to Washington, D.C. for the events surrounding January 6, 2021.

## January 6, 2021: Conduct at the U.S. Capitol (High-Level)

Accounts summarized in public reporting describe Rodriguez as being present in the crowd at the Capitol and engaging in violence during clashes with law enforcement. The most widely cited incident is the use of an electroshock device (stun gun) against Officer Michael Fanone.

## Legal Proceedings

Sentencing outcomes for January 6 defendants typically reflect the court’s assessment of:

## Why the Case Matters

Rodriguez’s case is frequently referenced in discussions of:
