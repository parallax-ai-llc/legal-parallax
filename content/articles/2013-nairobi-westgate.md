## Summary

Al-Shabaab gunmen attacked the Westgate shopping mall in Nairobi, killing 67 people in a four-day siege. Attackers reportedly used religious trivia to identify and separate non-Muslims.

## Background

- **Location**: Westgate Shopping Mall, an upscale retail complex in the Westlands area of Nairobi.
- **Context**: The attack occurred amid heightened regional security concerns linked to Somalia-based militant activity and Kenya’s military involvement in Somalia.

## Perpetrators and stated motive

- **Group**: The assault was attributed to **al-Shabaab**, an Islamist militant organization based in Somalia.
- **Claimed rationale (reported)**: Retaliation framing connected to Kenya’s security operations and presence in Somalia.

## Attack overview (high-level)

- **Start**: Armed attackers entered the mall and opened fire on civilians.
- **Siege**: The incident developed into a multi-day standoff involving Kenyan security forces.
- **Victim selection (reported)**: Some accounts describe attackers questioning hostages to determine religion (e.g., basic religious knowledge) and separating people accordingly.

## Immediate response

- **Evacuations**: Civilians fled or were escorted out as security forces worked to clear the building.
- **Multi-agency operation**: Kenyan police and military units participated in the response, with changing perimeter control during the siege.
- **Operational challenges (reported)**: Confusion over command structure and communication was widely discussed in contemporaneous reporting.

## Casualties and damage

- **Fatalities**: Commonly cited toll is **67 people killed**.
- **Injuries**: Large numbers of people were injured (exact counts vary by source/reporting date).
- **Property impact**: Significant damage to parts of the mall occurred during the attack and subsequent operations.

## Aftermath and investigations

- **Investigations**: Kenyan authorities pursued investigations into planning, facilitation, and security lapses.
- **Public debate**: The event triggered sustained scrutiny of counterterror readiness, intelligence coordination, and emergency response capacity.
- **Accountability**: Reviews and inquiries were discussed over time, with varied conclusions reported in different outlets.

## Broader impact

- **Security posture**: The attack influenced Kenyan domestic security policy discussions and mall/venue security practices.
- **Regional signal**: It underscored al-Shabaab’s ability to conduct high-profile attacks beyond Somalia.
- **Community effects**: The incident became a reference point in Kenya for inter-communal solidarity, trauma response, and memorialization.

- **Place**: Nairobi, Kenya (Westgate Shopping Mall)
- **Type**: Coordinated armed assault and siege
- **Attributed to**: al-Shabaab
- **Commonly cited death toll**: 67
