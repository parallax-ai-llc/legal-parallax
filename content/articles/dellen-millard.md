## Background

- Dellen Millard is a Canadian convicted murderer from Toronto, Ontario.
- He is often discussed alongside co-accused Mark Smich, who was convicted in related cases.
- Millard was described in reporting as having significant personal wealth and access to multiple properties.

## Known Crimes (High Level)

- **Laura Babcock (2012):** Millard and Smich were convicted in relation to her killing.
- **Tim Bosma (May 2013):** Bosma disappeared after a planned test drive of a truck he was selling online; Millard and Smich were convicted of first-degree murder.
- **Wayne Millard (2012):** Millard was convicted of first-degree murder for the death of his father, Wayne Millard, after the case was reinvestigated.

## Legal Outcomes

- Millard has been convicted of **three counts of first-degree murder** across separate proceedings.
- Public reporting commonly summarizes the sentence for first-degree murder in Canada as **life imprisonment with no parole eligibility for 25 years** (per count; parole eligibility rules can be complex when there are multiple convictions).
