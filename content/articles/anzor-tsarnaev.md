## Summary

Father of the Boston Marathon bombers who returned to Dagestan before the attack.

| Year | Event |

## Background

- Anzor Tsarnaev is known primarily through reporting on his sons, Tamerlan Tsarnaev and Dzhokhar Tsarnaev, who carried out the 2013 Boston Marathon bombing.
- Public biographical details about him are limited and vary across secondary sources; most accounts describe him as part of a Chechen diaspora family that lived across multiple post-Soviet regions.

## Family

- Spouse: Zubeidat Tsarnaeva (commonly identified in reporting as the mother of the Tsarnaev brothers).
- Children: includes Tamerlan and Dzhokhar Tsarnaev.

## Public Attention (2013)

- After the Boston Marathon bombing, Tsarnaev became a subject of intense media attention because of his relationship to the suspects.
- In interviews reported at the time, he was described as reacting with shock and condemning the attack, while also disputing aspects of the U.S. investigation and media coverage.

## Notes

- Because this topic is largely derivative of broader coverage of the Boston Marathon bombing case, details about Tsarnaev's personal life are best treated as secondary to the well-documented legal and historical record of the attack and prosecution.
