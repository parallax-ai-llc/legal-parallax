## Background

- **Full name**: Sean John Combs.
- **Also known as**: Puff Daddy, P. Diddy, Diddy (stage names used across different eras).
- **Primary fields**: Hip-hop and R&B (artist/producer), record-label executive, and entrepreneur.

## Notability (high level)

- Widely recognized for founding and leading **Bad Boy Records**, a label associated with major 1990s hip-hop/R&B releases.
- Known for producing and executive-producing recordings in addition to releasing music as a solo artist.
- Expanded into consumer brands and media projects alongside music.

## Additional Career Milestones (selected)

- **Early 1990s**: Emerged as a talent/label executive and producer in the U.S. music industry.
- **Mid-to-late 1990s**: Rose to mainstream prominence as both an executive (Bad Boy era) and recording artist.
- **2000s–2010s**: Continued work across music, touring, business ventures, and entertainment media.
