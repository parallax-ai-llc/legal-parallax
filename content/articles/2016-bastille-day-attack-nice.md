## Summary

Mohamed Lahouaiej-Bouhlel drove a 19-ton truck into Bastille Day crowds on the Promenade des Anglais for 2 km, killing 86. Over 430 were injured.

## Context

- **Date and place**: The attack occurred on **14 July 2016** in **Nice, France**, during Bastille Day celebrations.
- **Setting**: Crowds were gathered along the **Promenade des Anglais**, a major seaside boulevard used for large public events.
- **Type of incident**: It is widely described as a **vehicle-ramming attack**, a form of mass-casualty assault using a vehicle as the primary weapon.

## What Happened (High-Level)

- The attacker used a **large cargo truck** to drive through a dense crowd over a significant distance.
- The attack ended after a confrontation with police; the attacker was killed at the scene.
- French authorities treated the event as a **terrorist incident** and opened a major national investigation.

## Perpetrator (Commonly Reported Facts)

- **Name**: Mohamed Lahouaiej-Bouhlel.
- **Status**: Killed by police during the attack.
- Reporting on motivation and prior history varies across sources; official conclusions emphasize extremist influence and/or intent, while also noting complexities in personal background.

## Victims and Impact

- **Fatalities**: 86 people were killed.
- **Injuries**: Hundreds were injured (commonly reported as 400+).
- The attack affected **locals and international visitors** and became one of the deadliest attacks in modern French history.

## Immediate Response

- Emergency medical response and triage operations were carried out across the city.
- Authorities secured the area and coordinated victim identification and family support services.
- National leaders issued public statements and initiated state security measures.

## Investigation and Legal Aftermath

- Investigators examined planning, logistics, and potential accomplices, including how the truck was obtained and prepared.
- Subsequent proceedings in France addressed questions such as:
  - whether the attacker had assistance,
  - what networks (if any) were involved,
  - and how security procedures performed during a high-density public celebration.

## Policy and Security Significance

- The incident reinforced European concerns about **low-cost, high-impact attacks** that can be executed with everyday tools.
- It accelerated discussions and implementation around:
  - protective barriers for public events,
  - crowd-flow design and evacuation planning,
  - and intelligence-sharing on radicalization and threat indicators.
