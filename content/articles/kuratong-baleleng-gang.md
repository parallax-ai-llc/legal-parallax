## Overview

- The **Kuratong Baleleng Gang** is commonly described as an organized criminal group operating in the Philippines.
- Public discussion about the group is tightly linked to law-enforcement operations in the 1990s and the broader issue of police accountability.

## Reported Activities (High-Level)

Sources typically characterize the group as being associated with violent and profit-driven crimes such as:

- **Bank robberies**

## 1995 Police Operation and Controversy

- A major inflection point in the group’s public profile is a **1995 police operation** in which **11 alleged members** were reported killed.
- The incident has been described in reporting as controversial, including allegations that the deaths may have involved **extrajudicial killing** rather than a straightforward armed encounter.
- The event is frequently cited as a reference point in discussions of:
  - Use of force and rules of engagement
  - Evidentiary standards in major police actions
  - Institutional accountability mechanisms
