## Who He Is (Real-World Overview)

Azim Hasham Premji is an Indian business leader and philanthropist best known for building **Wipro** into a major information technology services company. He is widely recognized for large-scale charitable giving and for institutional philanthropy in education.

## Key Facts (High-Level)

- **Born:** 1945-07-24 (Bombay/Mumbai, India) [^2]
- **Known for:** Long-term leadership at Wipro; major philanthropic commitments through the Azim Premji Foundation [^2]

## Business & Leadership Notes

- Premji took over the family business at a young age after his father’s death and later led its transformation from a consumer goods manufacturer into a technology-focused enterprise (Wipro) [^2].
- Under his leadership, Wipro became an international IT services and consulting company, with growth driven by global demand for software and outsourcing services [^2].
- In later years, Premji transitioned day-to-day leadership to the next generation while remaining associated with the company’s long-term direction and governance [^2].

## Philanthropy & Social Impact
