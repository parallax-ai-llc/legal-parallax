## Summary

Russian billionaire who bought Trump's Palm Beach mansion for $95 million in a suspicious deal.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|

## Background

- **Origin:** Born in Perm (then Russian SFSR, Soviet Union). His parents worked at the Perm State Medical University (per Wikipedia).[^1]
- **Education:** Trained in medicine and initially worked as a doctor before shifting into business in the early post-Soviet period (per Wikipedia).[^1]

## Business & Wealth (high-level)

- **Core asset:** Uralkali (potash / fertilizer). Wikipedia describes Rybolovlev as becoming chairman in **1995** and later selling his majority stake in **2010** for a reported **$6.5B**.[^1]
- **Capital markets:** Wikipedia notes he obtained an early brokerage license in Russia and acquired stakes in various industrial companies in the Perm region during the 1990s.[^1]

## Sports Ownership

- **AS Monaco:** Wikipedia describes him as majority owner and president of **AS Monaco FC** from 2011.[^1]
- **Cercle Brugge:** Wikipedia lists him as the owner of Belgian club **Cercle Brugge KSV**.[^1]

## Art & Legal Disputes (Bouvier Affair)

- Rybolovlev is described on Wikipedia as one of the alleged victims in the **Bouvier Affair**, a dispute involving Swiss art transporter/dealer **Yves Bouvier** and the pricing/markup of high-value art transactions.[^1]

## Notes on the Palm Beach Mansion Transaction
