Public sources describe Joe Morgan (1929–1993) as one of the early leaders associated with the Mexican Mafia (also known as *La Eme*), a prison-based criminal organization that originated within the California correctional system.

Because reporting on prison gangs can vary by source, this page summarizes only high-level, widely repeated claims from mainstream references.

- Often characterized as a figure who helped establish the group’s internal hierarchy and discipline.
- Frequently mentioned in overviews discussing how leadership could project influence beyond prison walls through intermediaries.
