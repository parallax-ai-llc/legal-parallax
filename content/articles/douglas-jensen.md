## Background

- Jensen is a U.S. citizen from Iowa who became associated with the QAnon conspiracy movement.[^1]
- He drew national attention after being photographed inside the U.S. Capitol during the January 6, 2021 attack.[^1]

## January 6, 2021 (U.S. Capitol Attack)

- Video and photographs show Jensen wearing a distinctive orange-and-black Chicago Bears jacket while inside the Capitol.[^1]
- He was identified as part of the crowd that pursued U.S. Capitol Police Officer Eugene Goodman up a staircase, an episode that became a widely circulated symbol of the day’s violence.[^1]
- After the events, open-source images and reporting contributed to his identification and eventual arrest.[^1]

## Criminal Case (Federal)

- Jensen was charged in federal court in connection with his actions at the Capitol on January 6.[^1]
- In 2022, a jury convicted him of felony offenses related to the attack.[^1]
- The case is frequently cited as an early example of the U.S. Department of Justice pursuing felony convictions for January 6 participants based on video evidence and on-the-ground conduct.[^1]
