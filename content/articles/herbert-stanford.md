## Profile

- **Primary area of activity:** Laventille (an urban district in East Port of Spain, Trinidad).
- **Reported role:** Local gang leader / crime boss associated with street-level enforcement, intimidation, and control over drug distribution routes within the neighborhood.
- **Criminal markets typically tied to Laventille-era gangs:** Retail drug sales, control of “blocks,” armed enforcement, and associated extortion and reprisal violence.

## Context (Laventille)

Laventille has long been discussed in Trinidad and Tobago public-safety reporting as a community affected by poverty, high unemployment, and cycles of retaliatory violence. In that environment, neighborhood-level gangs often exert informal control over territory and street commerce, including narcotics distribution.
