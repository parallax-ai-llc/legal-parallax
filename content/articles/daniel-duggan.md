## Background

## Allegations and Charges (high level)

- U.S. authorities have pursued an extradition request and charges related to alleged unlicensed export of defense services.
- Reported charge categories include conspiracy (including to defraud the United States), alleged violations related to U.S. arms export controls (AECA/ITAR), and money-laundering-related counts.
- The public narrative around the case centers on alleged training of Chinese military pilots (including carrier-landing related skills).

## Legal / Procedural Notes

- Duggan has denied wrongdoing in public reporting.
