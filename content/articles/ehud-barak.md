## Background (high-level)

- **Military career:** Barak spent most of his early career in the IDF and later became Chief of the General Staff.[^1]
- **Political career:** After retiring from the IDF, he entered politics with Israel’s Labor Party and held senior cabinet roles, including Foreign Affairs and Defense.[^1]
- **Prime minister (1999–2001):** His tenure is commonly associated with major regional diplomacy efforts and domestic political turbulence around the early 2000s.[^1]
