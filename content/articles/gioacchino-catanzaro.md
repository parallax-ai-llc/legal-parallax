## Context: the 'Ndrangheta in Calabria

- The **'Ndrangheta** is a Calabrian organized-crime network whose local units are often described as **'ndrine** (family-based groups).
- It is commonly characterized in reporting and scholarship as having strong **kinship ties**, decentralized local structures, and an ability to coordinate across territories through alliances.
- Calabria (southern Italy) is frequently referenced as the group’s historic base, with influence reported in other Italian regions and abroad.
