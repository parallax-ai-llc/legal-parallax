## Background and Identity

- Krasimir Marinov is a Bulgarian figure commonly described in media and reference sources as part of a pair known as the **Marinov brothers** (“The Brothers”).
- He is frequently associated with Varna and with post-1990s organized-crime structures in Bulgaria.
- Publicly available biographical details are limited; many accounts focus on alleged influence rather than verified employment history.

## The Marinov Brothers and TIM (context)

- Reporting often links the Marinov brothers to the **TIM** group (also rendered as **ТИМ**), a powerful Varna-based network described variously as a security company, business conglomerate, and criminal organization depending on the source.
- In English-language summaries, “The Brothers” label typically refers to Krasimir Marinov together with his brother (commonly identified as **Nikolay Marinov**).
- Descriptions of TIM’s reach often include influence in:
  - private security and protection services
  - ports/shipping and transport-related businesses
  - hospitality and real estate
  - local political and economic patronage

## Alleged Activities and Influence (high-level)

- Sources generally characterize Marinov as an **alleged organizer/beneficiary** of illicit markets rather than a street-level actor.
- Commonly mentioned areas (varies by source; often framed as allegations) include:
  - racketeering/extortion and “protection” schemes
  - control over local businesses through intimidation or patronage
  - coordination with networks involved in smuggling or contraband trade
