## Identity and Disambiguation

Open sources commonly associate this name with **Abdishakur Sheikh Hassan Farah**, a Somali politician who served as Somalia’s **Minister of Interior Affairs and National Security** in the Transitional Federal Government (appointed **12 Nov 2010**).[^1]

This matters because the brief summary above describes an *al-Shabaab commander*; however, Wikipedia’s article for this exact title describes a **government minister** rather than a militant commander. Readers should treat the name as potentially **ambiguous** across different individuals and verify identity via role, dates, and affiliations.

## Death

Wikipedia reports that Abdishakur Sheikh Hassan Farah **died on 10 June 2011** in Mogadishu after being wounded in an attack at his home. The attacker was reported to be his teenage niece, who had been recruited by **al‑Shabaab**.[^1][^2]
