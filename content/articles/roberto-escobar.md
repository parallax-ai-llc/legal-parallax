## Background

- Roberto de Jesús Escobar Gaviria is widely described as the brother of Pablo Escobar.
- In many secondary accounts he is characterized as having handled accounting and financial logistics for the Medellín Cartel.
- Publicly available biographical details are limited and often repeated from the same small set of sources.

## Role and Reputation
