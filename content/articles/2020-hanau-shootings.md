## Summary

Far-right extremist Tobias Rathjen killed 9 people at two shisha bars in Hanau before killing his mother and himself. Published a racist manifesto before the attack.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|

## Context and Motive (high level)

- The attack occurred in **Hanau, Hesse (Germany)** on **19 February 2020**.
- It is widely described as a **far-right / racist** mass shooting targeting venues associated with immigrant communities.
- The perpetrator left behind writings that authorities and reporting characterize as **conspiracy-laden and racist**.

## Attack Overview

- **Two locations** were attacked in quick succession, including shisha bars.
- **Nine people** were killed at the venues.
- After the shootings, the perpetrator returned home and killed **his mother**, then **himself**.

## Immediate Aftermath

- The incident triggered large public vigils and national debate about **right-wing extremism** and public safety.
- German political leaders condemned the attack and framed it as an act of **terror** motivated by racism.

## What This Event Is Often Cited For

- Renewed scrutiny of:
  - online radicalization and extremist propaganda,
  - the adequacy of threat reporting and response mechanisms,
  - protections for minority communities and community venues.
