## Background and Education

- Born in Rome, Italy (Wikipedia lists 3 January 1966).[^1]
- Educated in Lausanne, Switzerland, before returning to Rome for university.[^1]
- Studied economics at LUISS University (Libera Università Internazionale degli Studi Sociali Guido Carli).[^1]

## Operating Career (Selected)

- **Procter & Gamble (from 1988):** Began in the French and Italian businesses as a brand manager.[^1]
- **Reckitt Benckiser (from 1992):** Held product/category roles and later senior European leadership; Wikipedia describes a multi-country career spanning ~16 years.[^1]
- **EMI Music (2008–2010):** Served as CEO of EMI’s recorded music division during a period of industry disruption.[^1]
- **Iglo Group (2013–2016):** Served as CEO of the European frozen food company Iglo; later associated with Nomad Foods around the Iglo sale (per Wikipedia summary).[^1]

## Investor and Board Roles (Selected)

- Co-founded **The Craftory** (2018), described by Wikipedia as an investment vehicle/fund focused on consumer “challenger brands.”[^1]
- Associated with **LSG/Leoni Sceti Group** (UK-based holding/investment interests are noted on Wikipedia).[^1]
- Board/affiliation examples listed by Wikipedia include **Kraft Heinz**, **Anheuser-Busch InBev**, **Barry Callebaut**, and non-profits such as **Room to Read** and **One Young World**.[^1]
