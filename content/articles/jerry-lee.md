## Espionage Case (High-Level)

- **Background:** Jerry Chun Shing Lee is a former CIA officer.
- **Allegations:** U.S. prosecutors alleged he conspired with Chinese intelligence and provided information that harmed U.S. operations in China.
- **Operational impact (reported):** The case is frequently discussed in the context of the **disruption of CIA human intelligence sources in China** in the 2010s, though public reporting varies on precise causality and scope.

## Investigation and Evidence (Public Reporting)

- The investigation has been described as involving **classified materials and operational notes** found in Lee's possession.
- Reporting on the case commonly notes that U.S. authorities reviewed his travel and contacts as part of the inquiry.

## Legal Outcome

- **Charge / conviction:** Convicted in U.S. federal court for **conspiracy** related to providing national defense information.
- **Sentence:** Public reporting notes a **multi-year federal prison sentence** following conviction.
