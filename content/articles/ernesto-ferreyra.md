### Femicide in Argentine law (high-level)

Argentina recognizes gender-based killing as an aggravated form of homicide in its federal penal code (commonly discussed under the term *femicidio*). In practice, this classification is used when the killing is tied to gender-based violence, often within intimate-partner or family relationships.

## Case significance (high-level)

Ferreyra is primarily noted for the legal outcome rather than for a public career: his conviction is cited as a representative femicide prosecution in Argentina. In broader discussions, such convictions are often referenced alongside:
