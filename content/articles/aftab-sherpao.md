## Background

- Born in Charsadda District (then in the North-West Frontier Province, now Khyber Pakhtunkhwa), Pakistan.
- Member of a prominent political family; his father was Abdul Akbar Khan (often referenced in Pakistani political history).
- Best known for roles in provincial and federal politics, particularly connected to Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

## Political Career (high level)

- Served in the Government of Pakistan as a **Federal Minister for Interior**.
- Served as **Chief Minister of the North-West Frontier Province (NWFP)** (now Khyber Pakhtunkhwa).
- Has been associated with mainstream national parties and later led/participated in regional political formations.

## Assassination Attempts

- Survived **two suicide bomb attacks** attributed to militant groups.
- The attacks are frequently cited in the context of Pakistan's mid-2000s security crisis and political violence.
- Public reporting describes him as a high-profile target due to his senior government roles, including the interior portfolio.
