## Historical Context (1971)

- Bangladesh’s 1971 Liberation War involved widespread violence and atrocities.
- Decades later, Bangladesh established domestic mechanisms to prosecute alleged crimes committed during the war.
- The most prominent forum for these prosecutions has been the International Crimes Tribunal (Bangladesh) (often abbreviated as ICT).

## Legal Proceedings (High-Level)

- Public summaries typically emphasize that the conviction occurred in 2013.

## Significance and Public Impact
