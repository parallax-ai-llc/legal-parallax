## Legal Case (High-Level)

- In 2014, he was **sentenced to life imprisonment** in relation to the murder of Clive "Lizard" Williams.
- In March 2024, the **Judicial Committee of the Privy Council** quashed the conviction, citing **juror misconduct**, and returned the matter to the Jamaican Court of Appeal regarding whether to order a retrial.
- On 31 July 2024, the Jamaican Court of Appeal **declined a retrial**, and he was **released from prison**.

## Cultural Impact (High-Level)

- Often described as a major, polarizing figure in modern Jamaican dancehall.
- Frequently discussed for the reach of his music beyond Jamaica and for influencing or being cited by international artists.
