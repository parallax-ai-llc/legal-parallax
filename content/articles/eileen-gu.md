## Background

- Born in 2003 and raised in the United States.
- Competed in freestyle skiing events including halfpipe, slopestyle, and big air.
- Represents China in international competition.

## Notable Achievements

- 2022 Winter Olympics (Beijing): two gold medals and one silver medal in freestyle skiing events.[^1]
- Multi‑time X Games gold medalist in freestyle skiing disciplines.[^1]
- Won medals at FIS Freestyle Ski and Snowboard World Championships (including gold).[^wg]

## Competition Highlights (high level)

- **Halfpipe:** Known for high amplitude and consistent landings in superpipe-style halfpipe formats.[^1]
- **Slopestyle:** Performs runs combining rail features with large jump tricks typical of elite slopestyle competitions.[^1]
- **Big air:** Competes in single-jump, trick‑execution formats where difficulty and landing quality are key.[^1]
