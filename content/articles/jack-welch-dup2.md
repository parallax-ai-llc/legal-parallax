## High-level facts (Jack Welch)

- **John Francis "Jack" Welch Jr.** (1935–2020) was an American business executive.
- He is best known for serving as **Chairman and CEO of General Electric (GE)**.
- Welch’s tenure at GE is frequently cited in discussions of late-20th-century U.S. corporate management and conglomerate strategy.

## Leadership themes commonly associated with Welch

- **Performance management:** Welch is associated with rigorous measurement and accountability practices.
- **Focus on competitiveness:** He emphasized ensuring businesses could compete strongly within their markets.
- **Capital allocation:** GE under Welch actively moved capital toward areas viewed as higher-return opportunities.
- **Management development:** GE became known for structured leadership development and executive training.

## Legacy (contextual, high-level)

- Welch remains a widely discussed figure in business education and management media.
