## Additional Context

### About the Sumiyoshi-kai

- Sumiyoshi-kai is one of Japan’s major yakuza syndicates (a large federation of affiliated groups).
- Like other yakuza organizations, it has historically operated through a mix of semi-public business fronts and illicit rackets.
- Reporting on internal titles, succession, and specific operational control is often fragmented and may vary by source.

### Typical Roles in Yakuza Organizations (High-level)

- **Kumichō (boss):** Overall leader of a syndicate or a constituent group; sets strategy and approves major decisions.
- **Wakagashira (underboss):** Senior deputy role; often oversees day-to-day coordination.
- **Shateigashira / senior executives:** Manage subordinate crews and regional operations.
