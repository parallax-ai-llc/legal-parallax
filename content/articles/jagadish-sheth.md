## Core Themes (High-Level)

- **Consumer behavior & buyer decision-making:** how individuals and organizations evaluate alternatives and choose.
- **Relationship marketing:** long-term customer relationships as an asset, not just transaction-by-transaction selling.
- **Customer-centric strategy:** aligning product, pricing, service, and communication around customer value and retention.
- **Global and emerging-market marketing:** adapting strategy across cultures, institutions, and market maturities.

## Notable Contributions (Broad, Factual)

- Helped shape the academic field of **marketing strategy** through research, teaching, and publication.
- Contributed to frameworks and vocabulary used in **relationship marketing** and customer relationship thinking.
- Served as a bridge between **academic research** and **practitioner strategy** through books, talks, and advisory roles.
