## High-level Biography (Giorgio Armani)

- **Born:** July 11, 1934 (Piacenza, Italy).[^2]
- **Known for:** Building one of the most influential modern luxury fashion brands, with a strong emphasis on clean lines and understated tailoring.[^2]
- **Business role:** Founder and long-time leader of the Armani group, overseeing multiple labels and product lines spanning apparel, accessories, cosmetics, and hospitality collaborations.[^2]

## Notability and Impact

- **Menswear & tailoring influence:** Armani is often associated with reshaping late-20th-century tailoring toward lighter construction and more relaxed silhouettes, particularly in menswear and power-dressing era suiting.[^2]
- **Film and celebrity dressing:** The brand gained global visibility through high-profile film costuming and red-carpet dressing, contributing to Armani’s international recognition.[^2]
- **Brand architecture:** The Armani name has been used across several diffusion and specialty lines (e.g., Giorgio Armani, Emporio Armani, Armani Exchange), reflecting a tiered brand strategy common in luxury fashion groups.[^2]
