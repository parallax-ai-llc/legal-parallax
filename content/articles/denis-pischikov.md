## Additional Details

### Known Aliases

- "The Shivering Creature" (Russian: "Тварь дрожащая")
- "Urals Raskolnikov" (nickname reported in some sources)

### Reported Crime Spree (per Wikipedia)

- **Span:** 2002–2003
- **Locations mentioned:** Moscow Oblast and Vladimir Oblast (Russia)
- **Victim profile (reported):** primarily elderly victims
- **Victim count (reported):** commonly cited as **14**, with some reporting a broader range (e.g., 14–17)

### Modus Operandi (high-level)

- Reported as combining **robbery/burglary** with lethal violence.
- Small amounts of cash and household items/food were reportedly taken in multiple incidents.

### Investigation and Arrest (high-level)

- He was **apprehended on 5 April 2003** (per Wikipedia).
- Reports describe a civilian noticing suspicious behavior and contacting police, contributing to his detention.
- After arrest, he reportedly **confessed** to multiple killings during the investigation.

### Trial and Sentencing

- **Sentence (reported):** life imprisonment.
- **Court outcome (reported):** life sentence issued in **July 2004**, later upheld on appeal.
- **Imprisonment (reported):** White Swan prison (IK-2) in Solikamsk, Perm Krai.

### Later Developments

- Some reporting states that in **2025** he confessed to additional murders; if substantiated in court, this could increase the total victim count.
