## Summary

## Enforcement and Controversy (high level)

- In 2022, Glencore-related entities (including Glencore Trading AG in U.S. filings) entered guilty pleas and settlements with U.S. authorities for conduct described as long-running bribery schemes involving intermediaries and officials in multiple countries.[^2]
- Public reporting and enforcement releases describe the misconduct as tied to commodity trading activities and payments designed to obtain or retain business and gain improper advantages.[^2]

## Key Outcomes

- **Criminal resolution (U.S.)**: DOJ announcements in 2022 describe a corporate guilty plea and monetary penalties in a global resolution.[^2]
- **Regulatory resolution (U.S.)**: The CFTC announced a parallel order and monetary sanctions related to bribery and manipulation controls issues in connection with commodity markets.[^3]
