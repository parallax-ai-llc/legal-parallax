## Background

- Khurram Butt was a British man connected to the 2017 London Bridge attack in London.
- Public reporting described him as being known to UK authorities prior to the attack.[^1]

## 2017 London Bridge Attack (Overview)

- The attack occurred on 3 June 2017 and involved multiple attackers.
- It began on London Bridge and continued in the nearby Borough Market area.
- The incident was treated as a terrorist attack by UK authorities and resulted in multiple fatalities and injuries.[^1]

## Role and Identification

- Butt was widely reported as a ringleader/leader figure among the attackers in the London Bridge incident.[^1]
- He was identified in media and official reporting following the event.[^1]

## Immediate Aftermath

- UK emergency services and counter-terrorism units responded rapidly on the night of the attack.
- The attackers were confronted and killed by police shortly after the attack began.[^1]
- The incident prompted heightened security measures and public safety messaging across London.[^1]

## Notability

- Butt is primarily notable for his association with the London Bridge attack, which became a major UK terrorism case of 2017.[^1]
- The attack had significant social and political impact in the UK, including renewed scrutiny of counter-terrorism efforts and extremist networks.[^1]
