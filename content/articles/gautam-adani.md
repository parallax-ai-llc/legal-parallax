## Business Profile

Adani’s rise is typically described as an expansion from commodity trading into capital-intensive infrastructure and energy businesses. The Adani Group has been associated with:

- port development and operations,
- power generation and transmission,
- broader infrastructure projects tied to India’s growth and industrial policy.

## Public Controversies

A major episode of international attention followed the publication of a 2023 report by **Hindenburg Research**, which alleged misconduct (including market manipulation and accounting issues) within the Adani corporate ecosystem. The group and associated parties publicly disputed key allegations, while the episode triggered sharp market moves and extensive public debate.

More broadly, Adani has been a recurring subject in conversations about:

- corporate governance and related-party structures,
- leverage and project-finance risk in infrastructure conglomerates,
- the relationship between large business groups and state policy.
