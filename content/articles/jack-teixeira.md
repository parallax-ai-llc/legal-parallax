## Summary

21-year-old US airman who leaked top-secret military documents about Ukraine war on Discord.

## Background

- Member of the Massachusetts Air National Guard and worked in an IT-related role supporting military communications systems.[^1]
- The leak drew attention because it involved contemporary battlefield intelligence assessments rather than historical archives.

## The 2023 Discord Leak (High-Level)

- Documents were shared in private Discord communities and later spread more widely online.[^1]
- The material included U.S. and allied assessments related to the Russia–Ukraine war and other national security topics.[^1]
- Open-source reporting noted that some images appeared to be photographs of printed briefing documents.

## Legal Proceedings

- Arrested in April 2023 after an FBI investigation identified him as the alleged source.[^1]
- Faced federal charges tied to the unauthorized retention and transmission of U.S. national defense information.[^1]

## Why It Mattered

- Prompted reviews of access controls for classified networks and internal monitoring practices.
- Became a prominent example of how small online groups can rapidly amplify sensitive information.
