## Summary

See Samy Amimour. French terrorist who was one of three suicide bombers at the Bataclan theater during the November 2015 Paris attacks. 90 people were killed at the Bataclan alone.

## Names and Identity

- **Full name**: Samy Amimour (commonly referenced with this given name in reporting).
- **Known for**: Role in the **13 November 2015** attacks in Paris, France.
- **Death**: Died during the Bataclan attack on **13 November 2015**.

## Context: The 13 November 2015 Paris Attacks

- The attacks involved multiple coordinated incidents across Paris, including:
  - The **Bataclan theatre** (concert venue)
  - Nearby cafés and restaurants
  - The **Stade de France** area
- The Bataclan attack was one of the deadliest components of the night.

## Role in the Bataclan Attack (High-level)

- Amimour is widely reported as one of **three suicide bombers** at the Bataclan.
- Attackers entered the venue during a concert and carried out a mass-casualty assault.
- The incident ended with the perpetrators dead after a police response and/or detonation of explosive vests.
