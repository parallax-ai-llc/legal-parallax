## Context (High Level)

- **El Mencho** is the commonly used alias for **Nemesio Oseguera Cervantes**, widely identified as a leading figure in Mexico’s **Cártel Jalisco Nueva Generación (CJNG)**.[^1]
- He is frequently described in reporting and law-enforcement statements as one of the most influential contemporary Mexican drug traffickers.[^1]
- Publicly available biographical details are limited and may vary across sources; this article keeps to high-level, broadly cited facts.[^1]

## Role and Organization

- Commonly characterized as a top leader of **CJNG**, an organization associated with large-scale drug trafficking and violent conflict with rivals and the state.[^1]
- CJNG is often discussed as one of the more prominent criminal organizations in Mexico in the 2010s–2020s.[^1]

## Selected Timeline (Non-Exhaustive)

- **2010s**: CJNG rises in prominence in Mexico; El Mencho is repeatedly named as a principal leader in open-source summaries.[^1]
- **2010s–2020s**: U.S. and Mexican authorities continue public manhunts and sanctions/reward programs connected to his capture (details vary by jurisdiction and over time).[^1]

## Notability and Impact
