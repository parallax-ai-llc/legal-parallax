## About Howard Hughes (canonical subject)

Howard Hughes was a prominent 20th-century American industrialist known for combining large-scale business operations with aviation and aerospace efforts.
He is widely associated with:

- Film production and Hollywood-era studio financing.
- Aviation engineering, record-setting flights, and aircraft development.
- Defense and aerospace contracting through later corporate structures.
- A later public reputation shaped in part by increasing reclusiveness.
