## Summary

Medieval pirate and mercenary who served both England and France before being captured and beheaded at sea.

## Background

- Born in the Boulonnais region (near Boulogne) in northern France.
- Originally associated with a monastic community (the nickname “the Monk” reflects this tradition), but later became a professional soldier and seaborne raider.
- Entered into the service networks of powerful nobles, including the counts of Boulogne, before shifting into private warfare at sea.

## Activity as a Raider and Mercenary

- Operated primarily in the English Channel, a strategic corridor for trade and military transport between England and northern France/Flanders.
- Worked as a mercenary captain: his ships and crews could be contracted to disrupt enemy shipping, escort convoys, or conduct coastal raids.
- Sources portray him as alternating between sides during the Anglo-French conflicts of the early 13th century, reflecting the era’s pragmatic (often transactional) military loyalties.

## Anglo-French Context (Early 1200s)

- His career is usually placed against the backdrop of King John’s reign, the loss of English Angevin territories in France, and the subsequent Franco-English struggle for the Channel.
- The period also overlaps with the First Barons’ War (1215–1217), when English internal conflict created openings for French intervention and intensified naval operations.

## Capture and Death (1217)

- In 1217, during the naval fighting connected to the Battle of Sandwich, he was captured at sea.
- Contemporary and later accounts emphasize his execution by beheading shortly after capture.
- His death is often cited as a notable episode in the consolidation of English control over the Channel during that phase of the war.

## Legacy

- Remembered as a prominent “pirate” figure of the medieval Channel, but many historians treat his actions as a blend of piracy and state-aligned privateering typical of the period.
- Appears in later storytelling traditions and popular history as an archetype of the renegade captain operating between kingdoms.
