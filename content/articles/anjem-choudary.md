## Background

- British Islamist activist and former solicitor (removed from the roll in the early 2000s, per public reporting).
- Became a prominent spokesman within the UK’s "al‑Muhajiroun" milieu, known for provocative public demonstrations and media appearances.

## Associated groups (commonly reported)

- **al‑Muhajiroun** (UK-based Islamist network; later proscribed/banned and rebranded under successor names).
- **Islam4UK**, **Sharia4UK**, and other successor/front organizations associated with the same network in media and research accounts.

## Legal status and prosecutions

- **2016 (UK)**: Convicted of **inviting support** for a proscribed organization (ISIS/ISIL), under UK terrorism legislation; sentenced to prison.
- **Post-release restrictions**: After release on licence, faced restrictions on public/media engagement for a period.
- **2024 (UK)**: Convicted of **directing a terrorist organization** (al‑Muhajiroun) and sentenced to **life imprisonment** with a substantial minimum term, according to widely reported court outcomes.
