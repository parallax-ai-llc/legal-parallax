## IKEA (high-level context)

IKEA is a Swedish-founded home-furnishings retailer known for:

- **Flat-pack (ready-to-assemble) furniture**, reducing shipping volume and cost.
- **Standardized, modular design** enabling mass production.
- **Self-service warehouse stores** and customer pickup.
- **Low-price positioning** supported by scale, logistics, and cost engineering.

These principles are commonly associated with Kamprad’s approach to retail operations and product distribution.
