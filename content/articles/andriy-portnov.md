## Expanded Profile

### Roles and background (high-level)

- Ukrainian lawyer and political figure who held senior positions during Viktor Yanukovych’s presidency.
- Widely discussed in Ukrainian and international media in connection with questions about influence over courts and law-enforcement institutions.

### Notable positions (as reported by public sources)

- Member of parliament (Verkhovna Rada) in the 2000s–early 2010s (associated with major political blocs of the period).[^1]
- Senior official in the Presidential Administration under President Viktor Yanukovych, with responsibilities linked to legal/justice-policy matters.[^1]

### Post-2014 period

- Left Ukraine after the 2014 revolution and remained a controversial public figure while abroad.[^1]
- EU restrictive measures were reported in connection with the post-Yanukovych investigations; some measures were later lifted/expired depending on court findings and evidentiary status.[^1]

### Sanctions and allegations

- In 2021, the U.S. Treasury announced sanctions designations tied to alleged corruption and influence-peddling regarding Ukraine’s judicial system.[^2]
- Allegations around court influence are contested topics in the public record; treat claims as allegations unless supported by adjudicated findings.[^1][^2]

### Why he is notable in this dataset

- Frequently cited as an example of the overlap between political power and judicial/constitutional processes in Ukraine’s pre-2014 governance.
- Mentioned in discussions of oligarchic and patronage networks and the durability of elite influence after the 2014 political transition.[^1]
