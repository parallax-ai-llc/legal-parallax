## Summary

Proud Boys member who smashed a Capitol window with a police shield on January 6.

## Background

- Dominic Pezzola is an American associated with the Proud Boys, a far-right extremist group.
- Public reporting primarily discusses him in relation to the January 6, 2021 attack on the U.S. Capitol.

## Role in January 6 (High-level)

- Prosecutors alleged that he used a police riot shield to break a window at the U.S. Capitol, helping create an entry point.
- The incident became one of the widely circulated visuals from the breach.

## Legal Proceedings (High-level)

- Pezzola was prosecuted in federal court as part of a set of Proud Boys-related cases.
- He faced charges that included assaulting law enforcement and other offenses arising from the events of January 6.
- Reporting indicates he was convicted of several charges at trial and received a significant federal prison sentence.

## Notability

- Often cited as an example of direct physical violence and property damage during the Capitol breach.
- Frequently referenced in discussions about accountability for January 6 participants.
