## Additional Context

### Background and Associations

- Deepak Tinu is described in reporting and reference summaries as a gangster from Punjab, India.
- He has been widely reported as being associated with organized-crime networks in the region, including the Lawrence Bishnoi gang.
- Public notability outside India is primarily tied to custody-escape incidents and linkage (as an accused/associate) in high-profile criminal cases.

### Custody Escapes

- The most-cited episode is his escape from police custody in 2022, which triggered a large manhunt and scrutiny of policing and prisoner-transport procedures.
- Accounts typically emphasize that the escape occurred while he was in transit under police guard, highlighting operational lapses rather than a prison break.

### Relation to the Sidhu Moose Wala Case

- Tinu has been named in public sources as linked to the 2022 assassination of Punjabi singer Sidhu Moose Wala.
- In most summaries, his role is framed as part of the network of accused individuals rather than the primary shooter(s).
