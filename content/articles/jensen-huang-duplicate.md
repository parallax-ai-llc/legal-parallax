## About Jensen Huang (primary subject)

Jensen Huang is a Taiwanese-American entrepreneur and the co-founder, president, and CEO of NVIDIA.
He is widely associated with the company’s long-running focus on graphics processing units (GPUs)
and, later, accelerated computing and AI infrastructure.

### Selected factual points (high level)

- Born in 1963 (Taiwan) and later moved to the United States. [^2]
- Co-founded NVIDIA in 1993 and has served as its CEO. [^2][^3]
- NVIDIA’s GPUs became central to 3D graphics in gaming and professional visualization,
  and later to general-purpose GPU computing and modern AI workloads. [^2][^3]
