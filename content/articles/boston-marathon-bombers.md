## Details

### What this entry refers to

This page is a high-level umbrella entry for the perpetrators of the 2013 Boston Marathon bombing.

- **Primary individuals**: Tamerlan Tsarnaev and Dzhokhar Tsarnaev.
- **Common usage**: The phrase “Boston Marathon bombers” is often used as a collective reference in media, public discussion, and some indexes.

### The 2013 Boston Marathon bombing (overview)

- **Location**: Boston, Massachusetts (near the Boston Marathon finish line).
- **Event**: Two pressure-cooker improvised explosive devices (IEDs) detonated during the marathon.
- **Immediate effects**: Fatalities and injuries; large-scale emergency response and investigation.

### Investigation and manhunt (overview)

- Identification of suspects involved public safety alerts and coordinated law-enforcement activity.
- The aftermath included a high-profile manhunt in the Boston area.

### Legal and historical significance

- The case became one of the most prominent U.S. domestic terrorism prosecutions of the 2010s.
- Dzhokhar Tsarnaev was tried in U.S. federal court and convicted in 2015.
