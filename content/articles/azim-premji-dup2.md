## High-level Profile (Azim Premji)

- **Known for:** Long-time leader of **Wipro Limited**, which evolved from a consumer goods company into a major Indian IT services and consulting firm.
- **Background:** Born in 1945; returned to India in 1966 after his father's death to manage the family business, later building it into a technology-led enterprise.[^2]
- **Reputation:** Often cited for combining professional management, long-term thinking, and a conservative approach to leverage with significant social investment.

## Key Themes

### Business and Leadership

- **Transformation:** Shifted Wipro's focus over decades toward software and IT services as India became a global hub for technology outsourcing.[^2]
- **Institution-building:** Emphasized governance, managerial depth, and continuity beyond a single founder.

### Philanthropy and Social Impact

- **Education focus:** Founded the **Azim Premji Foundation** (2001), centered on improving the quality and equity of school education in India.[^3]
- **Scale of giving:** Publicly pledged and transferred substantial Wipro shares for philanthropic purposes, placing him among the world's leading individual donors.[^2]

### Honors

- **Padma Bhushan (2005)** and **Padma Vibhushan (2011)**, among India's highest civilian honors.[^2]
