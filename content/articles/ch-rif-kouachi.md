## Background

- **Full name**: Chérif Kouachi.[^1]
- **Relationship**: Brother of Saïd Kouachi; the two are commonly discussed together in reporting on the January 2015 attacks in France.[^1]

## Early Life (high level)

- Born in France and grew up in the Paris region.[^1]
- Various biographies and press summaries describe a trajectory from an ordinary upbringing into later involvement with extremist networks.[^1]

## Radicalization and Networks

- Public sources describe his involvement in jihadist circles in France in the 2000s, including connections to individuals associated with plans to travel for militant activity.[^1]
- Accounts often mention a period of legal scrutiny and imprisonment related to these networks.[^1]

## 2015 Charlie Hebdo Attack (context)

- On **2015-01-07**, two armed attackers carried out the **Charlie Hebdo** shooting in Paris; Chérif Kouachi and his brother Saïd were identified as the perpetrators in widely cited reporting and subsequent summaries.[^1]
- The incident became one of the most consequential terrorist attacks in modern French history, triggering large public demonstrations and broad international political responses.[^1]

## Death

- Chérif Kouachi died on **2015-01-09** during a police operation in France, as widely reported in contemporaneous coverage and later summaries.[^1]
