## Summary
Eric Harris and Dylan Klebold carried out the Columbine High School massacre on April 20, 1999, killing 13 before killing themselves. Harris is considered the primary instigator.

## Early Life and Background
- Born in 1981 in the United States and grew up in a military family.
- As a teenager he lived in the Littleton, Colorado area, where Columbine High School is located.

## Relationship with Dylan Klebold
- Harris and Klebold were classmates and friends who participated in shared interests typical of local peers (including school and social activities).
- Their names are widely linked together because the Columbine attack was planned and carried out jointly.

## Lead‑up to the Columbine Attack (1998–1999)
- In 1998, Harris and Klebold were arrested for a theft incident and entered a juvenile diversion program.
- Both completed required counseling and community service elements associated with that program.
- Investigators later concluded that the pair prepared weapons and explosive devices in advance, with extensive notes and recordings described in official investigations.

## Columbine High School Attack (1999-04-20)
- On April 20, 1999, the two attacked Columbine High School in Littleton, Colorado.
- Thirteen people were killed and many others were injured; Harris and Klebold died by suicide at the scene.
- The event became one of the most studied school attacks in U.S. history due to its scale and extensive documentation.

## Investigation and Public Records
- Post‑attack investigations involved local law enforcement and multiple federal/state reviews; findings were compiled in official reports and supporting evidence releases.
- Public discussion has often drawn on official reports, court records, and contemporaneous media coverage.

## Legacy and Impact
- The Columbine massacre influenced school safety policies, threat assessment practices, and public debate about youth violence.
- It also had significant effects on media coverage norms (including later choices to limit repetition of perpetrators’ names and imagery).

## Notes on Attribution
- Many accounts portray Harris as a primary instigator, but the attack was planned and executed by both perpetrators.
- Details can vary across sources; official investigative reports are typically treated as the baseline for factual timelines.
