## Summary

A 22-year-old gunman killed 3 people and wounded 7 at the Field's shopping centre in Copenhagen. Denmark's worst mass shooting in decades.

## Background

- The attack occurred at Field's, a large shopping centre near central Copenhagen.
- Danish police described the incident as a shooting with multiple victims; authorities later stated there was no immediate indication of terrorism.

## Incident Overview

- Date: 3 July 2022.
- Location: Field's shopping centre (Copenhagen area).
- Casualties: 3 people killed; several others wounded.
- The suspect was apprehended shortly after the shooting following a police response.

## Victims

- The fatalities included civilians present at the shopping centre; public reporting identified two teenagers among those killed.
- People in the vicinity were advised to shelter in place during the initial response.

## Investigation and Legal Proceedings

- The case was treated as a criminal investigation; authorities assessed motive and the suspect's background.
- Subsequent proceedings focused on the suspect's criminal responsibility and long-term detention under Danish law.

## Aftermath and Public Response

- The shooting prompted national mourning and renewed discussion in Denmark about public safety and mental-health interventions.
- Public spaces and events in Copenhagen increased security presence immediately after the incident.
