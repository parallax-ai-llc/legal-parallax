## Background

- Reported to have been active primarily in Rajasthan, India, and described by police and media as a wanted gangster.
- Various reports describe a transition from local business and political activity into organized crime, alongside multiple criminal cases.

## Reported allegations and cases

- Public reporting commonly links him to allegations including murder and extortion.
- After periods of custody, he was reported to have escaped during transfer, which intensified a statewide manhunt and increased reward announcements.

## Police encounter and death (2017)

- He was killed on 24 June 2017 during a police operation described as an "encounter" in Rajasthan.
- The encounter narrative and circumstances were contested in public discourse: supporters and legal representatives alleged he intended to surrender and called for an independent investigation, while police statements maintained he was killed in an armed confrontation.

## Public reaction

- His death triggered significant protests in parts of Rajasthan.
- The protests were widely reported as involving community mobilization and demands for inquiry into the encounter.
