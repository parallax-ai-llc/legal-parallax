## Background

- **Nickname:** "Escadinha" (commonly rendered as *Escadinha* in English sources).
- **Country:** Brazil.
- Publicly known primarily for his association with **drug trafficking** and **prison escapes**, rather than for a conventional political or military role.

## Notability

- Widely cited for a **helicopter escape** from custody, an episode that became part of Brazilian crime folklore and is frequently referenced in later discussions of prison security.
- His notoriety is often framed as an example of how organized crime groups leveraged money, logistics, and corruption to defeat physical confinement.

## Helicopter Escape (overview)

- The best-known incident involves an escape using a **helicopter**, an unusually audacious method that required significant coordination.
- In many retellings, the escape is used to illustrate broader weaknesses in prison perimeter control and the challenges of preventing outside assistance.

## Later Life and Death

- Escadinha died in **2004**.
- Coverage after his death typically revisits the escape narrative as the defining moment of his public profile.

## Quick Facts

- **Known for:** Drug trafficking; helicopter prison escape.
- **Era of notoriety:** Mainly associated with the **1980s** in most summaries.
