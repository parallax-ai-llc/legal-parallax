## Incident Overview (2022)

High-level points commonly reported about the Brooklyn subway shooting (April 2022):

- The attack occurred on a New York City subway train in Brooklyn during morning hours.
- Smoke devices were reportedly used, creating confusion and reduced visibility.
- A handgun was used to fire multiple shots, injuring ten people.
- The attacker left the scene, leading to a large-scale manhunt.
