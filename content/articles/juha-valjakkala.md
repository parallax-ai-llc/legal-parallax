## Crimes and Investigation (High-Level)

- In 1988, three members of a Swedish family were killed in Åmsele.
- Swedish police investigated the murders and linked Valjakkala to the case.
- The case drew significant public attention in both Sweden and Finland.

## Trial and Sentence

- Valjakkala was tried and convicted in Sweden for the Åmsele murders.
