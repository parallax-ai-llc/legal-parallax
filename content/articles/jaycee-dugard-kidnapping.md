See Phillip Garrido. Jaycee Dugard was kidnapped at age 11 and held captive for 18 years.

## Background and context

- **Victim:** Jaycee Lee Dugard (born 1980), a child abducted in 1991 in South Lake Tahoe, California.
- **Abduction date:** 1991-06-10 (kidnapped while walking to a school bus stop).
- **Captivity:** Held for roughly **18 years** in Antioch, California.

## Key people

- **Phillip Craig Garrido:** Primary perpetrator; later convicted.
- **Nancy Garrido:** Phillip Garrido’s wife; also convicted for her role in the crimes.
- **Law enforcement / supervision:** The case involved failures in supervision while Phillip Garrido was under parole oversight (as widely reported in contemporary coverage).

## Aftermath and public impact

- The case became a widely cited example in discussions of **missing-person investigations**, **child abduction**, and **parole/probation oversight**.
- Dugard later published memoir and public statements describing survival, recovery, and rebuilding a life after long-term captivity.
