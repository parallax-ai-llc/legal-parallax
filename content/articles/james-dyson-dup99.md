## Why James Dyson Is Notable (High-Level)

- British inventor and entrepreneur best known for **popularizing bagless vacuum cleaners** using **cyclonic separation**.
- Founder of **Dyson Ltd**, a consumer technology company known for engineering-led products in home appliances and personal care.
- Frequently cited as a case study in **iteration, prototyping, and persistence** in product development.

## Key Contributions

- **Cyclone-based vacuum technology:** applying centrifugal forces to separate dust from airflow without a disposable bag.
- **Design + engineering commercialization:** building a premium consumer brand around performance, design, and industrial engineering.
- **R&D-driven product pipeline:** reinvesting heavily into research, prototyping, and manufacturing capability.
