## About George Soros (canonical subject)

George Soros (born 1930) is a Hungarian-born American investor and philanthropist.
High-level, widely reported points about Soros include:

- He survived the Nazi occupation of Hungary as a child.
- He later moved to the United Kingdom and studied at the London School of Economics.
- He built a career in finance, including founding Soros Fund Management and running hedge-fund strategies.
- He is known for large-scale philanthropy through the Open Society Foundations, supporting civil society and democratic governance initiatives.
